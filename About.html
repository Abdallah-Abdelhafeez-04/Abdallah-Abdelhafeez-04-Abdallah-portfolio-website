About Me

I'm Abdallah Abdelhafeez, a junior data analyst based in Egypt with a passion for turning raw numbers into powerful insights. My journey with data began during my early studies, when I discovered how a few lines of Python or SQL could uncover patterns hidden in complex datasets — and I haven’t stopped exploring since.

In just a few years, I’ve worked on projects ranging from employee performance analysis to predictive modeling and dashboard design. Whether it’s cleaning messy data, uncovering trends, or building machine learning models, I thrive on solving problems that combine logic with creativity.

The reason so many of my peers and mentors trust my work? I focus on clarity and impact — translating technical findings into clear visual stories that drive smarter decisions. I also bring a versatile skillset: Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and machine learning, ensuring every analysis is both accurate and actionable.

Curious to see what I can do? Explore my portfolio to discover projects, achievements, and services I offer. And if you’re ready to make your data work harder for you, feel free to get in touch!
